FAQ

Common Questions,
Honest Answers

How much does a new AC system cost?

Residential central AC systems typically range from $3,500–$12,000 installed, depending on system size, efficiency rating (SEER), and any ductwork modifications needed. We provide free, no-obligation quotes with transparent line-item pricing.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes. We partner with GreenSky and Wells Fargo to offer 0% APR financing for 12–18 months on qualifying installations. Applications take under 5 minutes and approval is instant in most cases.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

For emergency calls within the Tulsa metro, our average response time is 73 minutes. We operate 24/7/365 with no additional after-hours surcharge on weekends. Holidays are the only exception.

What brands do you install and service?

We're factory-authorized dealers for Carrier, Trane, Lennox, and Mitsubishi Electric. We also service all major brands including Goodman, Rheem, York, Bryant, and American Standard.

How often should I schedule maintenance?

Twice per year — once in spring before cooling season and once in fall before heating season. Our preventive maintenance plan covers both visits, priority scheduling, and a 15% discount on all repairs.

Do smart thermostats really save money?

Consistently, yes. Our customers average 22–34% reduction in HVAC energy costs after upgrading to an AI-learning thermostat. ROI is typically achieved within 8–14 months of installation.
